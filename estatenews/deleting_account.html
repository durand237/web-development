How to Deactivate your EstateNews Account

If you are looking to live the EstateNews plateform, then you are at the right place. Follow our step-by-step instructions on how to deactivate your EstateNews account.

Note: If you have an issue with your account (e.g problems to login, to edit your informations or potential account compromise) deleting your account will not help you furthrt. You can look in the EstateNews Support Center for the right solution. You can also contact us here.

Steps to Deactivate your estateNews Account

Step 1

Open the application and select the me menu in the bottom of the screen.

Step 2

If you are logged in, select the profile icon or the user are textfield below in the second box. If you are not logged in, you should log in first before going ahead. Dont't know how to log in to your account click here.

Step 3

From your User area scroll to the bottom of the page and click on delete my account

Step 4

Read the prompted message and confirm that you want to deactivate your account.

Deactivation FAQ.

We feel sorry that you choose to break your term with EstateNews and we wish to see you on our plateform again. The deactivation process will be done as fast as possible and all your post will no longer be available on our plateform except for those who saved it as favorite. We also want you to know that we will keep those post alongside with your informations for a period of 6 month before deleting them from our system. These is just a security guideline from us and there will be no extra task from you in these process.

Frequently asked Questions

- Can i create a new account after a deactivation?

Of course you can!! Right after the deactivation process you can reuse your email address to create a new account.

- Can i recover my account after a deactivation?

Yes!! You have have a period of 6 Month until the final deletion of your information. In this period of time you can still contact our support service per email at this address team@estate-int.com with the Object Account recovery. We will help you to fully recover your account.
